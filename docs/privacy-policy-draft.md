## Privacy Policy for Matron

**Effective date:** [EFFECTIVE DATE]

Matron is a client for a coding agent that runs on your own computer. You send
messages and files to that agent, and it sends back replies, code, and command
output. This policy explains what happens to that data.

### What Matron sends, and where

Matron talks to a Matron journal server. By default that is a server operated
by [ENTITY]. You may also point Matron at a server you run yourself, in which
case none of the data below reaches [ENTITY] at all, and the operator of that
server is whoever runs it — you.

When you use Matron with our server, the following is transmitted to and stored
on it:

- **Your email address**, used to sign you in and identify your account.
- **Your account identifier**, used to associate your sessions and devices.
- **Your messages** to and from the agent, including the conversation history
  shown in the app.
- **Attachments you send**: photos, files, and voice notes.
- **A device token** for each install that enables push notifications, so the
  server can wake the app when your agent replies.

Matron does not collect analytics, does not use advertising identifiers, does
not track you across other companies' apps or websites, and does not share your
data with data brokers.

### What stays on your device

Your conversation history is cached locally so the app works quickly and
offline. The local search index is stored with file protection enabled. Sign-in
credentials and recovery keys are stored in the system Keychain. Settings such
as your appearance choice and recently used folders are stored in local app
preferences and never transmitted.

### Agent access to your computer

Matron is a remote control for an agent running on your own machine. Anything
you ask that agent to do — read files, run commands, change code — happens on
your computer, under your control, and is not performed by [ENTITY]. Command
output and file contents that the agent sends back into a conversation are
stored with that conversation as described above.

### Voice notes and photos

Microphone access is used only to record a voice note when you choose to record
one. Photo library access is used only to attach a photo you pick. Neither is
accessed in the background, and neither is used for any purpose other than
sending the attachment you selected.

### How the data is used

Only to make the product work: delivering your messages to your agent and its
replies back to you, notifying you when something happens, and keeping you
signed in. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or sold to anyone.
